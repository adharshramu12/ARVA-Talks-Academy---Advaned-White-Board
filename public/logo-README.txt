PUT YOUR LOGO HERE
==================

Save the gold Arva Talks Academy eagle logo as:

    public/logo.png

It will then appear automatically:
  - on the landing page header
  - in the whiteboard top bar
  - as the browser tab icon (favicon)

Recommended: a square PNG (at least 256x256), transparent background.
Until you add it, a clean gold "A" mark + ARVA TALKS wordmark is shown instead,
so nothing looks broken.
